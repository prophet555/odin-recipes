Burrito

Description

A delicious meal of mexican origin. Can be made in under 20 minutes with healthy ingredients to energise you for the whole morning. Lots of protein.

Ingredients

  • pita
  • beef
  • coriander
  • taco sauce
  • cheese
  • beans

Steps

  1. Mix beef with taco sauce on fry pan
  2. chop up tomatos and wash coriander
  3. after 5 minutes add cheese and beans to the beef
  4. oven heat the pita bread
  5. put beef into bread add sour cream and jalepenos and serve with corriander
